This is an academic experiment on economic decision-making. To participate, you must be between 18 and 60 years old.

Setup Requirements

To participate in the experiment, you must:

  1. Use a laptop with a working front camera
  2. Sit at a desk
  3. Be alone in a quiet room

Camera & Lighting:
Please ensure your face is clearly visible to the camera by using sufficient and appropriate lighting. If you are near a window or natural light source, sit facing the light rather than having it behind you. If you're in a room without natural light, make sure the lighting is positioned in a way that illuminates your face. You can quickly test this using your front camera before continuing with the experiment.

Important:
