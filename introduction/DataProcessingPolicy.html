Data Protection Information and Consent to Data Processing

The data are collected exclusively for scientific purposes. The experimental data collected in this study are completely anonymous, i.e., they do not allow any conclusions to be drawn about your person, as identifying data collected for the payment of participation compensation is stored separately from the experimental data at all times.

The processing is carried out by researchers at the University of Göttingen (Chairs for Experimental Economics and for Affective Neuroscience and Psychophysiology). As part of the publication of the study results in scientific journals, the results of statistical analyses will be published in summarized form; conclusions about individual persons are always excluded.
